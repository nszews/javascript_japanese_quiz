Basic Japanese Vocabulary Quiz

Hi everyone! Ready to learn some Japanese? Let's get you started with some simple terms! Here we go!

  • わたし (pronounced "wa-ta-shi") means "I".
  • こんにちは (pronounced kon-ni-chi-wa) means "Hello"
  • ありがとう (pronounced a-ri-ga-tou) means "Thank you"
  • いいえ (pronounced ee-ay) means "No"
  • はい (pronounced hai) means "Yes"
